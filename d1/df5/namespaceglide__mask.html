real(dp) function glide_mask::calc_normal_45deg ( real(dp), dimension(3,3)  thck3x3)
Parameters
thck3x3Computes the angle of the normal vector, in radians, for the given 3x3 segment of ice geometry. The normal is given in increments of 45 degrees (no nicer interpolation is currently done) This is based on the Payne and Price GLAM code, if/when this is integrated into CISM it should probably be refactored to use this.
